Pasta Carbonara

Description

The best part about pasta is that it's so simple yet so delicious

Pasta Carbonara is like breakfast for dinner in pasta form.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (duh)
  • Eggs!
  • Bacon or Pancetta
  • Parmesan!
    • Half Parmigiano Reggiano
    • Half Pecorino Romano

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. crack half the eggs into a bowl
  3. crack the other half of eggs and only keep the yolk
  4. cook bacon in another pan
  5. bring it all together once done.
  6. Bon Appetit!
