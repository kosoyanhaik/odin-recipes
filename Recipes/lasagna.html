Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe has been approved by Italian grandmothers and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles alike.

Nothing comes close to how cheesy, carby, and tasty this recipe is.

Ingredients

  • Ribbon Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Your Choice of Toppings

Steps

  1. Buy instant lasagna.
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  3. Pop lasagna into oven, cook for 25 minutes.
  4. ENJOY!
